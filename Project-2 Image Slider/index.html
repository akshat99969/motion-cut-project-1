Lake Louise

Lake Louise is a glacial lake in Banff National Park, Alberta, Canada. Its turquoise waters and dramatic mountain backdrop make it one of the most photographed lakes in the world.

Lake Braies

Lake Braies, also known as Pragser Wildsee, is a lake in the Dolomites mountains in Italy. It's a stunning location with crystal-clear turquoise water, surrounded by towering mountains.

Tre Cime di Lavaredo

The Tre Cime di Lavaredo are a group of three distinctive, sharp-peaked mountains in the Dolomites of northeastern Italy. They are a popular destination for hiking and climbing.

Machhapuchhre,

Machhapuchhre, also known as "Fishtail", is a mountain in the Annapurna Himalayas of central Nepal. It is a popular trekking destination and is considered sacred by the local people.
